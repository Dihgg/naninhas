[h1]🧸 Naninhas - Plushie Buffs for Authentic Z[/h1]
[b]Naninhas[/b] turns [b]Authentic Z[/b] attachable plushies into active gameplay bonuses.
Attach a plushie to your backpack and gain its effect while equipped.

[h2]⚙️ How It Works[/h2]
[list]
[*]Attach a supported plushie to your backpack
[*]Its bonus activates while equipped
[*]Unequip it and the effect is removed
[*]Trait and XP effects are handled to avoid permanent leftovers
[/list]
